I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematical Economics in the Department of Mathematical Sciences at Aalborg University, working at the intersection of econometrics and risk modeling.

My research lies in time-series econometrics, focusing on resampling methods, high-dimensional model selection, and nonparametric methods for financial and macroeconomic data. I apply these tools, alongside modern machine learning techniques, to the analysis of financial and macro-financial risk.

I hold a PhD in Economics from Birkbeck, University of London.

Feel free to explore my [publications](/publications/), download my [CV](/cv/), or reach out via [email](mailto:charisios.grivas@gmail.com) or [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/charisios-grivas-a44614182).
